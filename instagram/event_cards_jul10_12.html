🛍️ Night Market
Portland Night Market
Portland's biggest night market fills SE Alder with 200+ local vendors — food, art, vintage, and handmade goods. Free and all ages, running until 11pm. Entry line can get long, but generally moves fast.

Oregon Public Shakespeare Festival presents Henry IV, Part 1 at Laurelhurst Park. Free outdoor Shakespeare — bring a blanket and enjoy the show under the evening sky. There's no Comedy in the park this week, so make this your Friday Laurelhurst Excursion!
Fri Jul 10 · Laurelhurst Park · 7pm · Free

Musicians play from front porches throughout the Overlook neighborhood as visitors walk house to house discovering sets. One of Portland's most charming free summer traditions. Look up the event map and schedule, and pair with the neighborhood yard sale!

Something I had been wanting to do ever since I moved to the PNW — I visited the Redwoods. I didn't have a ton of time so I did a quick loop near the Prairie Creek visitor center before heading north. My plan was to visit the Stout Grove the next morning, but there was a downed tree across the road and the bridge across the river was closed for the winter. So I pivoted and headed north to Oregon.
